“How do you take your coffee?”

The innocuity of this question is very unassuming but a response can speak volumes to one’s character. We do not play that game here.

Before Coffee.Press.Playing, understand that our purpose is to help you focus. We may provide info that may help you navigate the tribal and ritualistic world that is fancy shmancy pressed coffee, but we do not subscribe to or endorse any assholery that comes with the various coffee making paradigms out there. If you want to use angel tears, brought to a boil by the ohm’s of 100 unicorns doing yoga, by all means do it. If you have to use a spoon that was hand carved from the tree limb your great grandfather used to fight off a bear in the Himalayas, honor the sonofagun!. We also understand your coffee urges and would never judge if you brewed a cup using mystery grinds out of your left sock.
