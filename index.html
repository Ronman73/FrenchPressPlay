Soaking your grounds

The grounds you end up soaking will effect your coffee brew. The coarser the better. Common practice is to ensure all grounds are soaked for at least a minute. Which adds validity to R. Kelly's belief that there really ain't nothing wrong with a little bumpy grind.

Stirring is debatable. Do your research. Drink lots of coffee.

STEEP

Steeping is crucial. Our philistine palates prefer strong and stronger flavored coffee so we're not touching taste discussions. Dig deep into your soul to decide how long you want to steep to achieve your extraction preference. Our research (5+ online googled searched articles) agrees that less than 3 minutes will under extact and more than 6 will over extract. Choose wisely.

Less time = weaker/sweeter; more time = stronger/bitter.
